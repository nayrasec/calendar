How to Use Your Constellation 🌟

💾 Data Storage: All your memories are saved in your browser's localStorage. They persist even after closing the browser!

➕ Add Memory: Click "+ Add New Memory" to add a new date and story.

✏️ Edit Memory: Hover over a star and click the edit button to modify it.

🗑️ Delete Memory: Hover over a star and click the delete button to remove it.

⭐ View Details: Hover over any star to see the full memory details!
